My primary research interest is how message design strategies, especially framing, can differentially impact individualsâ€™ willingness to engage with political information. More specifically, I am interested in how message design can facilitate persuasion and reduce biased processing of ideologically motivated topics. Methodologically, I primarily work with online experiments, although I also do behavioral and psychophysiological (eye tracking) lab studies.
